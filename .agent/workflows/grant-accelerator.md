# /grant-accelerator - QLD Business Growth Fund

This workflow accelerates the submission of the **Registration of Interest (ROI)** for the QLD Business Growth Fund.

## CRITICAL DEADLINE: 30 January 2026, 5:00 PM AEST

### Step 1: Eligibility Verification
- [ ] **Turnover Check**: Verify FY2024-25 turnover is between $500,000 and $10,000,000.
- [ ] **Headcount Check**: Confirm fewer than 20 employees.
- [ ] **HQ Location**: Confirm main office is in Queensland.
- [ ] **Trading History**: Confirm at least 3 years of trading.

### Step 2: Strategic Alignment
The grant requires a project that involves purchasing **specialized equipment**.
- [ ] Identify equipment (e.g., CNC machines, advanced logistics software, medical equipment).
- [ ] Draft investment case (How will this scale the business?).
- [ ] Confirm 50% matched funding availability.

### Step 3: Document Preparation
- [ ] Fetch latest Profit & Loss from Xero.
- [ ] Fetch latest Balance Sheet.
- [ ] Generate "Growth Narrative" using AI logic.

### Step 4: Submit Registration of Interest
1. Navigate to [QLD Business Growth Fund Portal](https://www.business.qld.gov.au/starting-business/advice-support/grants/growth-fund).
2. Complete Stage 1 (ROI).
